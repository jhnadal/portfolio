Growing a SaaS Startup by improving the sign up flow

2021
Company / Product

Videnci is a Brazilian startup that offers B2B SaaS solutions to automate work routines by migrating checklists and workflows from paper to the cloud.

The project involved redesigning the company sign-up process to align with the Product-Led Growth (PLG) framework, which prioritizes a straightforward user experience without any internal barriers, allowing customers to complete tasks independently.

The original sign-up flow for Videnci required users to complete 12 separate screens to provide excessive information about their company. Error messages were unclear, and validation only occurred on the final screen, requiring users to manually backtrack to correct mistakes. The process took an average of 5 minutes to complete and had a low success rate of 7.69%, leading many potential customers to abandon the page due to the cumbersome experience.

Improvement points

We started by reviewing which fields the sales team used and eliminated any unnecessary information. We asked critical questions like why is each input there, what value does it bring, and is there an alternative way to get this information. After this analysis, we removed personal information and narrowed down to only essential fields: company name, segment, number of employees, customer name, phone number, and email address. We also simplified the options for segment and number of employees. Our design team reduced the number of screens from 12 to 2, making it easier for customers to provide information about their company and contact details.
